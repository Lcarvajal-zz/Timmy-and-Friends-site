Who we are

Timmy & Friends is an international nonprofit organization founded by young adults who wish to continue the legacy of Tim Kuhnert by improving the future of the mentally and physically impaired. It will accomplish this by emphasizing their integration into society, promoting rehabilitation, and advocating their fundamental rights.
